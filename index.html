🚂 Rail Frontier — How to Play

You run a young railway company. Connect towns and industries, move passengers and freight, and grow your empire.

  1. Find an opportunity — two towns close together, a coal mine (⛏) near a power plant (⚡), or a lumber camp (🪵) near a sawmill (🪚).
  2. Build stations (key 3) next to each — a station serves everything within 3 tiles (shown while placing).
  3. Lay track (key 2) connecting the stations. Click-drag to paint; trains run diagonals automatically. Track over water builds a bridge (expensive!).
  4. Buy a train (key 4): pick a type, click your stations in order, then Buy & Start.
  5. Trains loop their stops earning money on every delivery. Sawmills turn delivered wood into goods for towns, and well-served towns grow — more passengers, more money.
  6. Short on cash? Borrow from the bank in the Finance tab — watch the monthly interest.

Pan with right-drag / WASD / arrows. Zoom with the mouse wheel. Click the minimap to jump around. F follows the selected train. Space pauses. Esc cancels. The game autosaves every two minutes.

Click anywhere to close. Press H to reopen.
